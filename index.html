I am a post-doctoral scholar at the University of California, Berkeley, where I studied Mathematics and Mechanical Engineering and I was fortunate to work with Shawn Shadden.

I am passionate about making industry-level software packages on variety of platforms. I am the author of several python packages that can be found on PyPI and Anaconda Cloud, Node.JS apps that are can be found on npm-js, Docker images that are distributed on Docker Hub, and two high-performance computing web-servers that I developed and host at Berkeley Data Center which both are actively used by several research institutions world-wide.
